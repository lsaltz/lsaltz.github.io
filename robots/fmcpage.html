Follow-Me Car

I decided to build a robot car that follows me around like a puppy. I first used Fusion360 to CAD it. I worked with OpenCV's HOG to detect me and move the motors based on my position in the frame. Ultimately, using OpenCV, the robot was able to detect and track me, and the motors moved accordingly.

See the code here

Its brain is a Jetson Nano, and it uses a Waveshare motor hat.
